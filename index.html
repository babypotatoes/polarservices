the 3 steps to getting pol/ar

contact support for a download

email us at support@polarservices.ga to enroll in the beta
program and receive a unique download link.

use your link to download pol/ar

pol/ar and all of its required assets is built into one .exe file which
contains a unique password specific to the user it has been assigned to by support.

put pol/ar in an easy to find place

move the pol/ar exe to some place on your computer that
you find the most convenient. The best places are usually your desktop or shortcutted from your program files.
